About Startup@Berkeley

Startup@Berkeley connects students at the University of California, Berkeley to the greater entrepreneurial ecosystem. Members of ST@B have gone on to found a number of their own companies, including Ness, Copilot, CrazyEgg, and KISSmetrics.
