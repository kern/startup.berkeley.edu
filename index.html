About Startup@Berkeley

Startup@Berkeley connects student entrepreneurs with the University of California, Berkeley's startup ecosystem through curated online resources. Members of ST@B have gone on to found a number of their own companies, including Ness, Copilot, CrazyEgg, and KISSmetrics.

For more information regarding Startup@Berkeley, how to get involved on campus, and how to get your event or organization listed, please feel free to reach out.
